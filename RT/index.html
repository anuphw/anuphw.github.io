Some definitions

Whenever Arsenal looses ball possession in-play, I record speed and acceleration of each player towards their own goal line (X-direction) two seconds after loosing the ball possession.

I have 5 types of plots for both Speed and Acceleration of players, 2 seconds after loosing the ball. The types are as follow:

  1. Speed Acceleration When ball is lost, player is between ball and own goal.
  2. Speed Acceleration When ball is lost, player is between ball and opposition goal.
  3. Speed Acceleration When ball is lost, player is in own half.
  4. Speed Acceleration When ball is lost, player is in opposition half.
  5. Speed Acceleration All observations.
